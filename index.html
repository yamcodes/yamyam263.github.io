Handasaim Schedule

Overview

There was once a time when every Handasaim student had a daily struggle: having to download the schedule to their device, everyday. And then luz was created...

luz (officially: Handasaim Schedule) displays a redesigned daily schedule taken from Handasaim. The result is an re-imagined online schedule customized for each class.
